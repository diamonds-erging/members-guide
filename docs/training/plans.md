# Training Plans

## The All-round Pete Plan

The [Pete Plan](https://thepeteplan.wordpress.com/the-pete-plan/) is recommended by many of our most active members. This aims to develop all-round rowing ability, and is capable of being adjusted as your rowing prowess develops.

## British Rowing

British Rowing offer a number of plan that target the gold standard 2000m or the shorter, faster 500m events for rowers with different levels of experience and ability. So whether you're a beginner, intermediate, advanced or even adaptive rower, there's [a plan for you](https://insideindoor.com/training/training-plans/).

## Rod's 10k Training Plan

For those who like longer distances, our club captain Rod Chinn offers the a [five-week 10k-specific plan](rods-10k-plan.md).
