# Workout of the Day

Concept2 offer a [workout of the day](https://www.concept2.co.uk/training/wod), every day, to be done on the RowErg, SkiErg or BikeErg.

These are short, varied and fun workouts, usually taking less than 30 minutes, and designed to work for a wide rnage of ability and experience.

Each workout comes with a honorboard that can be filtered to match your performance to those in the same age, weight category and gender.

You can sign up to their email list to have the daily challenge sent to your mailbox each morning.
