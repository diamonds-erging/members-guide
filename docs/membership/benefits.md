# Benefits of Membership

## Fellowship

Indoor rowing and erging n general can be a very isolating form of exercise. By affiliating to the Diamonds you become part of the World's largest rowing club, and part of a team with over 3,000 members.

## Participation in World Record Attempt

Each season we aim together to row more metres than the previous season. In coming season we're aiming collectively to exced 3 billion metres, which would be a new World Record. If we succeed, a personalised certifcate will be available to every member who has contributed to our total.

## Annual Certificate of Achievement

Many members like to rank their performances for the standard indoor rowing events in their Concept2 logbook. At the end of each season a personalised certicate is available for each member listing their achievements of the previous year including national (where available) and world rankings for each event.

## Access to Erging Expertise

Those who subscribe to the Facebook group receive advice and encoragement in improving their fitness and erging peformances, and can request and participate in discussions about topics of special interest.

Members who aren't subscribed to this group can receive a weekly email updating them on the progress toward the World Record and motivating hints and tips to assist with your owing journey.
